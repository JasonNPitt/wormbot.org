Hardware Tutorials

The WormBot is built out of the platform of the MakeBlock XYplotter robotics kit. From a harware standpoint the WormBot simply replaces the drawing or engraving components of the XYplotter with a camera. The resulting robot is then fitted with a transillumination arm to image the worms with optimal contrast and the robot is affixed to an optically clear table that contains 12 slots to hold microtiter plates.
